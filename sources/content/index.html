About C2M2

Welcome to Collections of Cinema and Media Music, or C2M2, a guide to library collections of film and media music. C2M2 is currently in development and we welcome suggestions for resources to be included in the database. You may also contact us with general questions or comments or read more about our plans for C2M2.
