About C2M2

Welcome to Collections of Cinema and Media Music, or C2M2, a guide to archival collections of film and media music.

C2M2 is currently in development. To see the current state of the project, feel free to visit the development site or browse our code repository on GitHub.

We welcome suggestions for resources to be included in the database. You may also contact us with general questions or comments or read more about our plans for C2M2.
